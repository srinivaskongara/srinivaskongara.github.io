Technology changes quickly. Human curiosity does not.

June 2026

Over the years, I have worked across .NET, cloud, and AI, helping people use technology, build technology, and learn technology.

While the tools continue to evolve, one thing remains constant: people learn through curiosity, experimentation, and shared knowledge.

I am grateful for the knowledge others have shared with me, and this site is my way of sharing some of that knowledge in return.

This site is a journal of ideas, observations, and lessons learned along the way.
